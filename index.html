About Blossom & Co.

Founded in 2015 by Maya Patel, Blossom & Co. began as a small stall in a weekend market. What started as a love of color and scent quickly grew into a full-service florist studio serving weddings, local businesses, and families. Maya trained in botanical design and apprenticed with several master florists before opening the studio. From day one, sustainability and community have been at our heart: we partner with local growers, reduce waste where we can, and donate blooms to hospitals and shelters when large events finish.

Our Values

  1. Sustainability: We prioritize seasonal, locally grown flowers and compost our waste whenever possible.
  2. Craftsmanship: Each design is assembled by experienced florists who understand balance, line, and rhythm.
  3. Community: We teach free monthly workshops and support neighborhood events with floral donations.
  4. Care: Freshness guarantee and a simple returns policy to ensure delight with every delivery.

Flower Care — Make Blooms Last

Proper care extends the life of your flowers by days or even weeks. Follow these general tips and the specific notes included with each bouquet.

General Steps for All Cut Flowers

  1. Start with a clean vase and fresh, cool water.
  2. Trim stems at a 45° angle with sharp scissors or shears — remove about 1–2 cm from the bottom.
  3. Remove leaves below the water line to prevent bacterial growth.
  4. Use the flower food packet included in your delivery — if you don’t have one, a teaspoon of sugar and a few drops of lemon juice can help.
  5. Change the water every 2–3 days and trim stems slightly each time.
  6. Keep blooms away from direct sunlight, drafts, and ripening fruit.

Care Notes for Popular Flowers

Roses

Remove the outer petals if they look damaged. Roses benefit from a slightly warmer room and weekly stem trims.

Orchids

Store in bright, indirect light and avoid overwatering. They prefer humidity but not direct sun.

Tulips

Tulips continue to grow in the vase — give them plenty of height and a tall, narrow vase.
